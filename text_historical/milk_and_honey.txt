PLACES AROUND TOWN

<ol><li>Schoolyard - trees, some big stones, maybe a few tree-swings</li>
<li>Empty lot behind the feed store - sometimes there are horses here</li>
<li>Berry field north of town</li>
<li>Church/School/Town Hall - all the same building, perhaps</li>
<li>Pine Ridge Cemetery</li>
<li>Water Tower/Well</li>
<li>Mill River swimming hole</li>
<li>Jameson's Mile - straight road out to the Jameson place, good for racing</li>
<li>Willow Lane - a path by the river, favored for clandestine meetings</li>
<li>Town Common - green pasture in the center of town, used for small livestock of in-town dwellers</li>
<li>Sugar House - a long barn used seasonally to boil maple sap into syrup</li>
<li>Prisoners Row - stocks, jail, and gallows</li>
<li>Old Man Henderson's orchard - the sweetest fruit, if you can get it</li>
<li>Checker boards and benches outside the feed store - here's where the news really is</li>
<li>Mrs. Davis' backyard laundry business - always has clothes on the line</li>
<li>Hobb's Corner - place where that fellow got shot last year</li>
<li>Stagger's Mine - abandoned for years</li>
<li>The room over the millinery shop - smoking and gambling for men</li>
<li>Hermit's lean-to - three-sided cabin used by hunters etc., left provisioned for whoever comes next</li>
