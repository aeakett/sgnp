MEDIEVAL FRENCH

Compiled by Shreyas Sampat

NOTES

These are Norman and Occitan French names. When the Normans invaded Britain they brought their language with them, so this could also be suitable for a game set there; use French names for the gentry and Germanic names for the common people.
