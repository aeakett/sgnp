<p class="contributor">Compiled by Moyra Turkington</p>
<h2>NOTES</h2>
<p>The following Maori names are chosen from an unpublished manuscript compiled about 1925 by the missionary Rev. Henry James Fletcher (1868-1933). Thanks to the university of Waikato for making it available online. In this era, (the late 1800's - early 1900's) surnames were only beginning to be introduced among the Maori. The surname taken would generally be the first name of the person's father.</p>
