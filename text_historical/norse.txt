<h2>NOTE</h2>
<p>Eth and thorn characters (ð, Ð, þ, Þ) are common in Anglo-Saxon, old Scandinavian, and modern Icelandic and Faroese.  They look really exotic and cool.  Pronounce them like "th" and don't worry about it.  </p>

SURNAMES
Create surnames by adding "-son" or "-dottir" to a parent's name.  Thus, Rúnar son of Berg, becomes Rúnar Bergsson and Fjóla, daughter of Brigitta, becomes Fjóla Brigittasdottir.
